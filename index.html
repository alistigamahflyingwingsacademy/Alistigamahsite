Rules and Regulations

  • Adherence to regular payment of fees and forms dues.
  • Parents/Guardians should be vigilant to their children/wards, concerning punctuality.
  • Obtain the school Uniform for the section as prescribed in the attachment.
  • No applicant would be allowed to attend classes without being duly registered.
  • Cell-Phones and lunch money is not allowed, rather food and the like should be provided before stepping into the school premises. The applicant may subscribe to the school kitchen.
  • No student should leave the school before closing without a prior genuine reason from the parent/guardian to the concerned staff.

NB:

  • School uniform should be obtained from the school.
  • Class placement is based on performance and age.
  • Any issue that has not been captured within the jurisdiction of the school, Islamic injunction would be applied.
  • Returning this form means accepting of the above mentioned rules and regulations.
