Mission

Al-Istiqaamah strives to develop an enriched learning environment that promotes academic achievement, leadership and Islamic values, nurturing learners who live by the Qur’an and the Sunnah while contributing positively to society.

Vision

To cultivate a community of believers and learners who embody faith, knowledge, and inspiration---becoming beacons of guidance, excellence, and service to humanity.

📌 Core Objectives

Qur'anic & Prophetic knowledge

Joyful memorization

Principled leadership

Rules and Regulations

  • Adherence to regular payment of fees and forms dues.
  • Parents/Guardians should be vigilant to their children/wards concerning punctuality.
  • Obtain the school uniform for the section as prescribed.
  • No applicant would be allowed to attend classes without being duly registered.
  • Cell-Phones and lunch money are not allowed; food should be provided before stepping into the school premises.
  • No student should leave the school before closing without a genuine reason from the parent/guardian.

NB:

  • School uniform should be obtained from the school.
  • Class placement is based on performance and age.
  • Returning this form means accepting the above rules.
